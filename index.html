ABOUT WILSON BELTS

    

Wilson Belts is a premium championship belt manufacturer based in Sialkot, Pakistan — the world's capital of sports goods manufacturing. Founded by Asad Ali, every belt is handcrafted with precision CNC metalwork, 24k gold plating, and genuine cowhide leather straps.

    

We produce WWE, WWF, TNA, UFC, Boxing, and fully custom championship belts in both 2mm and 4mm plate thickness. Each piece is made to order and shipped worldwide. Quality guaranteed — straight from Sialkot to your door.
