ABOUT WILSON BELTS

    

Wilson Belts is a premium championship belt manufacturer based in Sialkot, Pakistan — the world capital of sports goods manufacturing. Founded by Asad Ali, every belt is handcrafted with precision metalwork and genuine leather straps.

    

We produce WWE, WWF, TNA, AEW, UFC, Boxing, and fully custom championship belts. Each piece is made to order and shipped worldwide. Quality guaranteed — straight from Sialkot to your door.
